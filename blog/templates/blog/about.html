About

181hub is a social website that lets users share thoughts and content freely. No restrictions what so ever. Users are allowed to create posts with video, audio and images. Any adult and unauthorized use of copyrighted content will be taken down if reported

How to use 181hub

Sigining Up and Loging In: you are allowed to create an account or sign in with Gmail or Facebook if you wish to sign in with other social website account

For Mobile Device.

For PC Click on the Log In .

Previous Next
Create Post: logged in users can create posts. When creating a post you can upload upto four pictures, one video(.mp4) and one audio(.mp3)
Previous Next
Like Post: logged in users can like a post by taping the icon
Share Post: logged in users can share a post with their followers by taping the icon
Save Post: logged in users can save a post by taping the icon
Mentions: a post containing another account's username, preceded by "@" symbol. For example: "Hello @jago", In the recipient's notification tab, the mention will appear in the recipient's Home if they are following the sender
Hashtags: logged in users can use the hashtag symbol(#) before a relevant keyword or phrase in their post. Clicking or tapping on a hashtagged word in any message shows you other posts with similar hashtag
